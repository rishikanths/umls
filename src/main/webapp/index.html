Hierarchy View tab renders all the childern (isa relationship) of the search term.
For example, Apple 'isa' Fruit and Mango 'isa' Fruit, that means Apples and Mango are children of Fruit or Fruit is the parent of Apple and Mango.

Relation View tab renders all the relationships (other then isa relationship) of the search term.
For example, Fever 'maybe_treated_by' Advil, that means the terms Fever and Advil are related using 'maybe_treated_by' relationship.
